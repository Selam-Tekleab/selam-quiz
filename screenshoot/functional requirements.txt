	Functional requirements

Ability to add a new contact with fields for first name, last name, company, job title, one or more phone numbers, and one or more email addresses.

Ability to update existing contacts' information, including first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, and email addresses.

Ability to delete contacts from the application.

Ability to search for contacts based on first name, last name, company, job title, phone numbers, or email addresses.

Ability to merge duplicate contacts, ensuring that no redundant information is present in the application.

Ability to display contacts in a human-readable format, such as JSON.

Ability to handle errors gracefully, such as invalid input or missing contact information.
